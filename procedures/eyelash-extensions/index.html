Eyelash Extensions: No More Mascara!

You’re tired of wearing mascara every day to make your eyelashes look full and long. It’s a hassle, it takes time, and you can never seem to get the right amount on each lash.

Imagine waking up in the morning with beautiful lashes that are already curled and ready for you. Your eyes will be brighter and more awake-looking, and people will notice how pretty they look!

Eyelash extensions are applied one lash at a time by trained professionals who use special tools to apply individual synthetic hairs directly onto your natural lashes. The result is an instant transformation without any mess or fuss – just gorgeous-looking eyes!

How Does the Eyelash Extensions Procedure Work at Natural Sparkle Beauty Parlours?

Before Treatment

As you come in for your appointment, our team will assess your lashes to get an understanding of the condition they’re in and what type of extensions would be best suited for you.

We will also discuss the different lash extension options we offer and the length, thickness, and colour of the lashes you would like to achieve.

During Treatment

Once you have decided on the perfect lash look, our team will apply the extensions one by one to your natural lashes. A complete set of lashes takes approximately 90 minutes to apply. You can maintain them year-round with recommended touch-ups every two to three weeks. 

They can be used as filler to make your natural lashes thicker or applied outward from the eye for something different.

After Treatment

When your lash extension treatment is complete, you’ll be able to enjoy the benefits of beautiful lashes that are ready to go – no need for mascara!

Your lash extensions will last anywhere from two to three weeks, at which point you’ll need a fill to maintain the look. Fills are recommended every two to three weeks to keep your lashes looking their best.

If you want to take a break from lash extensions, simply let your natural lashes grow out, and they will eventually fall off on their own
